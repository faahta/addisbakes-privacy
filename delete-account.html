Delete My Account

You can request the deletion of your Addis Bakes account and all associated personal data using the form below. We will process your request within 7 business days and send a confirmation to your contact details.

After submitting, you will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours. Your account and data will be fully deleted within 7 business days.

Alternatively, you can email us directly at fasso143@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and your registered phone number.
